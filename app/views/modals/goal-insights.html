Section 1: My Personal Goal
Write down one personal goal you would like to achieve in the next 6-12 months
1. To what degree am I motivated toward this goal?
2. Why am I persuing this goal?
3. How will I achieve this goal?
Section 2: Evaluating the motivation framework of my Person
Instructions: Slide the circle on the continuum where it represents how strongly the goal is connected to each motivation.
1. Intrinsic Motivation
The desire to do something just for the experience itself.
2. Identified Motivation
Required to do but internalising into a sense of self, seeing the value and doing it with personal volition
3. External Motivation
Activities we are forced to, we see little value in and are not fun
4. Introjected Motivation
You are the forcer, guilt tripper, pusher
Actions
What actions could you take to make the intrinsic and identified motivations stronger?
What actions could you take to make the external and introjected motivations weaker?
